## Stripe

Client's can sign up for an account at [stripe.com](https://stripe.com/).

Then they can invite the developer to the account with the "Developer" role. 

Instructions for the client [here](https://support.stripe.com/questions/invite-team-members-or-developers-to-access-your-stripe-account).

```txt
Please sign up for a Stripe account at https://stripe.com/.

Then you can invite me to the account with the 'Developer' role.

Instructions to do that are here: 

https://support.stripe.com/questions/invite-team-members-or-developers-to-access-your-stripe-account
```
